About Us

About Us

Capital Hill Barber Shop & Styling Salon has become somewhat of a Helena institution. From its beginning as a small barbershop in the Capital Hill Shopping Center in 1965 to the present, our shop has grown & thrived.

Capital Hill Barber Shop & Styling Salon has stood the test of time. Over its 54 years, other barbershops and salons have come and gone. Our dedicated staff has helped us adapt to the changing need of the Helena community, at the same time adhering to our tradition of friendly, quality service.
